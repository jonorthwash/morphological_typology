How does it work?

You will be presented with the following graph, there will be two axes: Analytic—Synthetic and Fusional—Agglutinative. The Analytic—Synthetic axis relates to the number of morphs (or morphemes) per token, you may define token as you see fit, as word or as construction, but the unit should be comparable for the languages you choose. The Fusional—Agglutinative axis relates to the number of "meanings" per morph (or morpheme). Examples: Klingon, Quenya and Valyrian.
